Rules of Shoprab

1. Every visitor begins with 10 Void.

2. Enhancements (powers) cost Void and demand an equal sacrifice.

3. You must choose at least 5 powers before proceeding.

4. Sacrifices are permanent and cannot be undone.

5. You may not exceed your total Void balance.

6. Every power changes you — sometimes subtly, sometimes completely.

7. Choose wisely
